Frequently Asked Questions

At Imprimis IVF Srinagar, the cost of a complete IVF cycle ranges from ₹1,20,000 to ₹2,00,000, including all medications, monitoring, egg retrieval, embryo transfer, and lab fees. The exact cost depends on your specific treatment protocol. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden charges.

A complete IVF cycle typically takes 4-6 weeks from the start of ovarian stimulation medications to the pregnancy test. This includes approximately 10-14 days of stimulation, egg retrieval, embryo development (3-5 days), and the two-week wait before testing.

Our live birth success rates are 55-65% for women under 35 and 40-45% for women aged 35-40. These rates are approximately 1.5× the national average, reflecting our advanced lab technology and experienced clinical team.
