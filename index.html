All 11 Fertility Treatments

Every treatment we offer, explained in detail.

IVF (In Vitro Fertilization)

The cornerstone of assisted reproduction — eggs are fertilized with sperm in our advanced lab, and the healthiest embryo is transferred to the uterus. Ideal for blocked tubes, endometriosis, or unexplained infertility.

ICSI

Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection — a single healthy sperm is injected directly into each egg. The gold-standard treatment for severe male factor infertility.

IUI

Intrauterine Insemination — washed, concentrated sperm is placed directly into the uterus during ovulation. A gentle, affordable first-line treatment.

Egg Donation

Carefully screened egg donor programme for women with diminished ovarian reserve or genetic concerns. Complete confidentiality.

Sperm Donation

Rigorously screened sperm donors, following ICMR guidelines. For severe male infertility or single women seeking parenthood.

Embryo Freezing

Vitrification technology preserves surplus embryos with excellent post-thaw survival rates for future family building.

Laparoscopy

Minimally invasive surgery to diagnose and treat endometriosis, cysts, fibroids, and tubal blockages.

Hysteroscopy

A tiny camera examines the uterine cavity to treat polyps, adhesions, septum, or fibroids — ensuring optimal implantation.

PGS/PGD (Genetic Testing)

Embryos are tested for chromosomal abnormalities before transfer, increasing implantation rates and reducing miscarriage risk.

Male Infertility Treatment

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment: semen analysis, hormonal assessment, varicocele repair, and advanced sperm retrieval.

PCOS Management

Personalized care for PCOS — combining lifestyle counselling, ovulation induction, and advanced fertility treatments.
