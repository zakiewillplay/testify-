Our Fertility Treatments

11 advanced services. One compassionate team. Everything you need under one roof in Rajbagh, Srinagar.

IVF (In Vitro Fertilization)

The cornerstone of assisted reproduction — eggs are fertilized with sperm in our advanced lab, and the healthiest embryo is transferred to the uterus. Ideal for blocked tubes, endometriosis, or unexplained infertility.

ICSI

Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection — a single healthy sperm is injected directly into each egg. The gold-standard treatment for severe male factor infertility, including low sperm count or poor motility.

IUI

Intrauterine Insemination — washed, concentrated sperm is placed directly into the uterus during ovulation. A gentle, affordable first-line treatment for mild infertility and donor sperm cycles.

Egg Donation

Our carefully screened egg donor programme offers hope to women with diminished ovarian reserve, premature ovarian failure, or genetic concerns. Complete confidentiality is maintained throughout.

Sperm Donation

Access to rigorously screened sperm donors for couples facing severe male infertility, azoospermia, or single women seeking parenthood. All donations follow ICMR guidelines.

Embryo Freezing

Vitrification (rapid freezing) technology preserves surplus embryos with excellent post-thaw survival rates. Ideal for family planning, fertility preservation before medical treatments, or future sibling cycles.

Laparoscopy

Minimally invasive keyhole surgery to diagnose and treat endometriosis, ovarian cysts, fibroids, and tubal blockages. Performed by our expert surgical team with rapid recovery times.

Hysteroscopy

A tiny camera examines the uterine cavity to diagnose and treat polyps, adhesions, septum, or fibroids — ensuring the optimal environment for embryo implantation.

PGS/PGD (Genetic Testing)

Preimplantation Genetic Screening and Diagnosis — embryos are tested for chromosomal abnormalities or specific genetic conditions before transfer. Increases implantation rates and reduces miscarriage risk.

Male Infertility Treatment

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment of male factor infertility, including semen analysis, hormonal assessment, varicocele repair, and advanced sperm retrieval techniques (TESA, PESA).

PCOS Management

Personalized care for Polycystic Ovary Syndrome — combining lifestyle counselling, ovulation induction, and advanced fertility treatments to help women with PCOS achieve healthy pregnancies.
