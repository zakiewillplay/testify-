Our Fertility Specialists

Two dedicated doctors. One mission: turning your hope into a heartbeat.

Dr. S. S. Lone

MD, DNB – Obstetrics & Gynaecology
Fellowship in Reproductive Medicine

"Every child is a miracle worth fighting for."

Dr. S. S. Lone is a senior IVF specialist with over 15 years of experience in reproductive medicine. After completing his MD and DNB in Obstetrics & Gynaecology, he pursued advanced fellowship training in Reproductive Medicine at a leading national institute. He has performed over 3,000 IVF/ICSI cycles with consistently high success rates. Dr. Lone is known for his meticulous approach, ethical practice, and the genuine compassion he brings to every patient consultation. He specializes in complex infertility cases, recurrent IVF failure, and advanced embryo transfer techniques. Under his leadership, Imprimis IVF has become the most trusted fertility centre in the Kashmir Valley.

Dr. Shabeena Lone

MS – Gynaecology
Diploma in Ultrasound

"I believe in turning heartbreak into hope."

Dr. Shabeena Lone is an expert in high-risk pregnancies and fertility preservation. With her MS in Gynaecology and a Diploma in Ultrasound, she brings precision diagnostic skills to every case. She has a special interest in managing PCOS-related infertility, recurrent pregnancy loss, and fertility preservation for women undergoing medical treatments. Dr. Shabeena is the clinic's lead for ultrasound monitoring during IVF cycles, ensuring optimal timing for egg retrieval and embryo transfer. Patients consistently praise her warm bedside manner and her ability to explain complex medical concepts in simple, reassuring terms. Together with Dr. S. S. Lone, she forms the compassionate backbone of Imprimis IVF Srinagar.
